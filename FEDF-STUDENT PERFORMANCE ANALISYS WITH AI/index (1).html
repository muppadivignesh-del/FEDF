About the Project

Built to bridge the gap between data and decisions

Student Performance Analysis is designed to reduce the manual effort traditionally required in evaluating academic records. By automating the calculation and display of key performance metrics, it saves time and minimizes errors.

The system helps teachers make informed, data-backed decisions — identifying students who need extra support before they fall behind, and recognizing high achievers who deserve encouragement.

  • Two dedicated dashboards — one for students, one for teachers
  • Students can only view their own scores and performance
  • Teachers get full class analytics and every student's data
  • Role-based access with secure email domain validation
  • Simple data processing techniques, accessible to all educators
